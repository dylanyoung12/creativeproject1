Welcome!

Have you ever had a terrible roommate? I know I have. Don't you wish there were some way to avoid these kind of situations?

Well now there is! Welcome to RateMyRoommate, the website for warning others about terrible roommates, or letting the world know about good ones. Or, you can just take out your frustrations for the whole Internet to hear.

Those familiar with Rate My Professors should be able to find their way around here just fine.

So, what are you waiting for? Go ahead and get started!
